# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We pledge to make participation in this project a respectful, inclusive, and constructive experience.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and patient.
- Assume good intent.
- Give actionable feedback.
- Keep discussions focused on the project.
- Respect privacy and confidentiality.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks.
- Publishing private information without consent.
- Introducing confidential financial or customer data into issues, pull requests, or examples.
- Deliberately disruptive behavior.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, remove, or reject contributions that violate this code of conduct.
